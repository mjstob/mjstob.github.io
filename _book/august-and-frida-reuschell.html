Myrtle Reuschell

Chapter 3 August and Frida Reuschell

August had immigrated to the states as a young boy and still lived with his family at the Thomas St. residence in 1900. However, on April 19, 1902 he married Frida Erpel, the girl from across the street.

August and Frida quickly had two children. Myrtle was born on December 17, 1902 and Howard (known as Mike) was born on June 4, 1904. By 1910 they were living at 1833 N. Sawyer. August first worked for his father in the bakery and then had his own bakery. Frida passed away in 1919 at the age of 38. Her daughter Myrtle quit school and went to work in her father’s bakery which at that time was in the Lawndale neighborhood at 3947 W Roosevelt Road.

Myrtle married Abel Folgers in 1923 and Mike married Frances Kutzok in 1934.

August died a tragic death on May 25, 1929. Driving in car with his brother Fred, Fred’s boy Freddie, and one other man, he tried to beat a train at a railroad crossing and the car was struck killing all four. The crash made the front page of the Chicago Tribune and a photo of the wreckage appeared inside.
