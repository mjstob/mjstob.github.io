Chapter 2 Charles and Marie Erpel

Charles Erpel was born in Berlin on August 13, 1850. Marie Hendrickson was born in Schleswig-Holstein in 1857. They married in Schleswig-Holstein in 1877 and had three children in Germany, Paulinea, Max, and Frida. Charles immigrated to Chicago in 1881 and Marie followed in 1882 with the three children. By 1887, they were living at 1344 W. Division with the three children and were eventually to have three more children, Clara, Emil and Fred. By 1900, Charles, Marie, and the children were living at 2615 Thomas, just across the street from the Reuschells.
